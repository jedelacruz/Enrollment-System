Welcome to ubldaycare

At ubldaycare, we believe in providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for your child to grow and learn. We invite you to come and visit our facility and see for yourself why ubldaycare is the perfect place for your child to begin their educational journey.

Enrollment Process

Enrolling your child at ubldaycare is easy! Simply fill out our online application form and one of our staff members will be in touch to schedule a tour of our facility. During the tour, you will have the opportunity to meet our teachers and staff, learn more about our programs, and ask any questions you may have. Once you have decided to enroll, we will provide you with all the necessary paperwork and information to ensure a smooth transition for your child.

Tuition and Fees

We understand that choosing a preschool for your child is an important decision, and we strive to make our programs as affordable as possible. Our tuition and fees vary depending on the program and schedule you choose. We offer full-day and half-day care options, as well as before and after school care for children attending local elementary schools. Please contact us for more information about our current rates and availability.
